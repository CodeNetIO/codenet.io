Hone your skills and acquire new abilities by undertaking a wide range of complex and interesting challenges. Learn from or teach other developers with a customized, personal knowledgebase tied directly to your dojo solutions.

Features Of Codenet

  • Unit Tests

    Built-in Unit tests for each Kata challenge provide explicit acceptance criteria.

  • Performance Considerations

    Performance is a factor in ranking kata, however, readability and maintainability are graded by the community.

  • Skill Tracking

    Track and display your skills as you grow. Completing kata results in experience that increases your level as a developer.
